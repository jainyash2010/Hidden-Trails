Famous Places

Discover the most iconic landmarks and attractions across Haryana

Raja Raghu's Fort (Jind)

Historic fort dating back to the Mahabharata era.

Panipat Museum

Showcases artifacts from the three battles of Panipat.

Sheikh Chilli's Tomb (Thanesar)

17th century monument dedicated to Sufi saint Sheikh Chehli.

Brahma Sarovar (Kurukshetra)

Sacred water tank where Lord Brahma created the universe.

Mansa Devi Temple (Panchkula)

Famous Shakti Peetha temple in the Shivalik foothills.

Nada Sahib (Panchkula)

Important Sikh Gurudwara on the banks of Ghaggar river.

Sultanpur National Park

Bird watcher's paradise with over 250 species of birds.

Damdama Lake

Largest natural lake in Haryana with adventure activities.

Kalesar National Park

Dense forest with diverse flora and fauna near Yamuna river.

Kingdom of Dreams (Gurugram)

India's first live entertainment and leisure destination.

Ambience Mall (Gurugram)

One of India's largest shopping and entertainment complexes.

Cyber Hub (Gurugram)

Premier dining and entertainment destination in Cyber City.

Famous Food

Savor the authentic flavors of Haryana's cuisine

Bajra Khichdi

Nutritious millet-based dish cooked with lentils and local spices.

Haryanvi Kadhi

Yogurt-based curry with besan pakoras, served with rice or bajra roti.

Ghewar

Unique desert preparation cooked , a Jind specialty.

Kachri Ki Chutney

Spicy chutney made from wild cucumbers, perfect with meals.

Bathua Raita

Yogurt with bathua leaves, a winter specialty in Haryana.

Haryana's Mixed Dal

Five types of lentils cooked together with local spices.

Gur ka Halwa

Traditional sweet made with wheat, ghee and jaggery.

Mithe Chawal

Sweet rice preparation, a festive specialty in Haryana.

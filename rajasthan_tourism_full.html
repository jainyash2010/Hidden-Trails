Famous Food

Savor the authentic flavors of Rajasthan's cuisine

Dal Baati Churma

Nutritious millet-based dish cooked with lentils and local spices.

Gatte Ki Sabzi

Yogurt-based curry with besan pakoras, served with rice or bajra roti.

Ker Sangri

Unique desert bean preparation cooked with spices, a Udaipur specialty.

Mirchi Bada

Spicy chutney made from wild cucumbers, perfect with meals.

Mawa Kachori

Yogurt with bathua leaves, a winter specialty in Rajasthan.

Bhujia

a crispy snack prepared by using Vigna aconitifolia, gram flour and spices.

Ghevar

Traditional sweet made with wheat, ghee and jaggery.

Balushahi

Sweet rice preparation, a festive specialty in Rajasthan.
